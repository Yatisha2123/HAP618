Data Analysis based on Extent of Glycaemic Control, using the HbA1c Measured Values


The data analysis was done using the Python Libraries of Pandas, Numpy and Matplotlib to create Bar Graphs to visualize the data of Diabetes Mellitus with respect to the Extent of Glycaemic Control, using the HbA1c Measured Values. The data was divided on the basis of each Demographic Specification, namely Males, Females, Whites, African-Americans and Hispanics. Based on this, new data frames were located and created to plot bar graphs. The data was formed into Arrays having the Years, and the axis for the labels were set. On the other hand, for the Demographics of Federal Poverty Levels and Age, the data was first grouped based on the specified ranges using the Define: If, Else If and Else functions. After this, the data was plotted for the bar graphs.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2018 for Males.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2016 for Females.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2017 for Whites.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2018 for African-Americans.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2017 for Hispanics.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2017 for people below 200% Federal Poverty Level.
The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2016 for people in between 201-300% Federal Poverty Level.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2016 for people above 300% Federal Poverty Level.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2015 for people in between 20-40 Years of Age.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2016 for people in between 41-60 Years of Age.

The Extent of Glycemic Control for Diabetes Mellitus was most effective in the Year 2017 for people above 60 Years of Age.
